About

Ms. Riya Chhabra, daughter of Mr. Gurjeet Singh Chhabra, is a seasoned expert with more than 12 years of experience in real estate, mall operations, and hotel management. At 35 years old, she holds an MBA and plays a crucial role in shaping the operational and marketing strategies for the group’s hotel ventures.
