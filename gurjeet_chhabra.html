Mr. Gurjeet Singh Chhabra

About

Mr. Gurjeet Singh Chhabra, also known as Pintoo Chhabra, is a prominent figure in the real estate sector in Indore. At 60 years of age, Mr. Chhabra is a B.A. graduate with over 30 years of experience in construction, infrastructure maintenance, and real estate development. His extensive background includes developing landmark properties and managing a diverse portfolio. Under his leadership, the group companies achieve an average EBIDTA income of approximately Rs. 180 Crore annually, encompassing rent, CAM, interest, and other operational revenues.

Mr. Chhabra's journey began with the successful operation of renowned marriage gardens in Indore, including RIA GARDEN and KARAN GARDEN. Recognizing the retail boom in tier II towns, he transitioned into mall development, leading to the creation of CENTURY 21 TOWN PLANNERS PVT. LTD. and M.P. ENTERTAINMENT & DEVELOPERS PVT. LTD.

In 2015, Mr. Chhabra took over MP's First Mall, known as "TREASURE ISLAND (TI MALL)," situated in Indore’s heart. His most recent achievement includes the completion of the C21 BUSINESS PARK in April 2019, a prestigious project with nearly 100% occupancy by reputable brands and MNCs. Today, Mr. Chhabra is a key owner of several landmark properties in Indore, including C21 Mall, Malhar Mega Mall, Radisson Blu, Radisson Red, TI Mall, Best Price, Effotel, Sheraton Grand, Le-Meridien Surat, and Amber Garden.
